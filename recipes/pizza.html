Pizza

Description

A classic homemade pizza with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water; let sit 10 min.
  3. Mix in flour, salt, and olive oil to form dough. Let rise 30 min.
  4. Roll out dough, place on a baking sheet.
  5. Spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add toppings.
  6. Bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
