Lasagna

Description

A layered Italian classic with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella—perfect for family dinners or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar pasta sauce (24 oz)
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook noodles and set aside.
  2. Brown beef, drain fat, and mix with pasta sauce.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, parmesan, salt, and seasoning.
  4. Layer in a 9x13 pan: sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with remaining cheese. Cover with foil.
  6. Bake 25 minutes covered, then 10 minutes uncovered until bubbly and golden.
