Garlic Butter Pasta

Description

Simple and flavorful pasta tossed in garlic butter and herbs—perfect for a quick, comforting meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente; drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Toss pasta in the garlic butter. Add salt, pepper, and parsley.
  4. Top with parmesan and serve hot.
